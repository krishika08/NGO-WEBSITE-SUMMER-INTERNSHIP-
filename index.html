AAS SOCIAL WELFARE SOCIETY

- Bringing Smile Through Education & Empowerment

Established in 2002, AAS Social Welfare Society is a grassroots development organization dedicated to empowering communities and transforming lives. Every year, our initiatives touch the lives of over 20 lakh children and their families across India. With more than 400 dynamic projects focused on education, healthcare, livelihood, and women empowerment, AAS operates in more than 2,000 remote villages and urban slums spanning 27 states.

We act as a catalyst for sustainable change, working in synergy with government missions and local efforts to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals. Through collaborations with like-minded individuals and organizations, we implement high-impact programs that improve access, elevate quality, and foster long-term behavioral change at the grassroots level.

OUR WORK

EDUCTION FOR THE POOR

AAS Social Welfare Society is committed to providing quality education to underprivileged children, helping them build a brighter and more empowered future.

BLOOD DONATION CAMP

AAS Social Welfare Society organized a blood donation camp to encourage voluntary blood donation and help save lives. Many people participated in this noble cause, showing unity and compassion.

WATER DISTRIBUTION

AAS Social Welfare Society conducted a water distribution drive to provide clean drinking water to underprivileged communities, especially during extreme heat. This small act brought great relief and smiles to many.

AMBULANCE SERVICE

AAS Social Welfare Society provides free ambulance services to support patients in medical emergencies. This service ensures timely help for the underprivileged, saving lives when every second counts.
